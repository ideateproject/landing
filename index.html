Ideate is the student entrepreneur's complete platform for networking, advising, and access to valuable resources.

We bring entrepreneurs together and give them the tools they need to thrive. Here's how we do it:

Community

Need a teammate who can compliment your skillset? Want to share a cool project with your classmates? Use our platform to connect with other entrepreneurs on your campus and build something amazing! Think of it like a dating app for founders and other innovators.

Content

Engage with our numerous content partners who offer curated resources specific to the college student. Topics range from interviews with industry leaders to virtual events through your school.

Funding

Check out our database of dozens of venture funders (including school pitch competitions and alumni angel networks) looking to invest in products from students like you. The best part? It only takes one submission to reach as many as you want!
